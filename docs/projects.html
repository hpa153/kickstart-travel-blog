South Korea: An unexpected charm of Seoul

Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a huge metropolis where modern skyscrapers, high-tech subways and pop culture meet Buddhist temples, palaces and street markets. Seoul is a vibrant, enchanting city with two main palaces – Gyeongbokgung and Changgyeon.

Taiwan: Memorable from Taipei to Kaohsiung

Taiwan has a truly unique natural beauty. The subtropical volcanic island is more than 70% towering, jagged mountains that in some places rise nearly straight out of the Pacific Ocean. Volcanic activity has created numerous hot springs and cavernous lava rock coastlines on Taiwan.
